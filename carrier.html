    Dispatch Service Agreement
    Supporting the Backbone of American Freight.

    This Dispatch Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between GraniteSky Dispatching ("Dispatcher") and the undersigned Carrier ("Carrier").

    By signing below, the Carrier agrees to the following terms and conditions.


    1. SERVICES PROVIDED
    GraniteSky Dispatching agrees to provide dispatch support services, which may include:

    Searching and securing freight opportunities
    Negotiating freight rates with brokers and shippers
    Scheduling pickups and deliveries
    Communicating with brokers, shippers, and receivers
    Completing broker setup packets
    Sending rate confirmations
    Assisting with appointment scheduling
    Monitoring shipment progress
    Providing general dispatch support throughout transportation
    GraniteSky Dispatching acts solely as an independent dispatch service and is not a licensed freight broker, motor carrier, or transportation provider.


    2. CARRIER RESPONSIBILITIES
    Carrier agrees to:

    Maintain active MC and DOT operating authority.
    Maintain all required insurance throughout this Agreement.
    Operate in compliance with all applicable federal, state, and local laws and regulations.
    Deliver freight safely and on time.
    Notify Dispatcher immediately of delays, breakdowns, accidents, or other issues affecting scheduled loads.
    Provide Proof of Delivery (POD) immediately after delivery.
    Maintain professional communication with brokers, shippers, and receivers.
    Carrier remains solely responsible for the safe operation of its equipment, compliance with regulations, cargo security, and all transportation services performed.


    3. DISPATCH FEE
    Carrier agrees to pay GraniteSky Dispatching a dispatch fee equal to 8% of the gross amount of each load booked by GraniteSky Dispatching, unless another written agreement has been executed.

    The dispatch fee becomes earned when the Carrier accepts a load that GraniteSky Dispatching secures and confirms on the Carrier's behalf.


    4. PAYMENT
    Invoices will be issued according to the billing schedule established between Dispatcher and Carrier.

    Carrier agrees to pay all invoices by the due date shown on the invoice.

    Late payments may result in suspension of dispatch services until the account is brought current.


    5. INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR STATUS
    GraniteSky Dispatching is an independent contractor.

    Nothing contained within this Agreement creates a partnership, employer-employee relationship, joint venture, or agency relationship between the parties.


    6. NO GUARANTEE OF LOADS
    GraniteSky Dispatching will make reasonable efforts to locate freight opportunities.

    However, Dispatcher does not guarantee:

    Any minimum number of loads
    Any minimum revenue
    Specific freight lanes
    Particular freight rates
    Broker acceptance
    Freight availability depends on market conditions, broker requirements, and Carrier qualifications.


    7. CANCELLATION
    Either party may terminate this Agreement by providing written notice to the other party.

    Termination does not affect GraniteSky Dispatching's right to receive dispatch fees for loads booked and accepted before the termination becomes effective.


    8. CONFIDENTIALITY
    Both parties agree to keep confidential all proprietary business information exchanged during the course of this relationship, including customer information, broker contacts, pricing information, and business practices, except where disclosure is required by law.


    9. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
    GraniteSky Dispatching provides dispatch support only.

    Dispatcher is not responsible for:

    Cargo loss or damage
    Equipment damage
    Accidents
    Mechanical failures
    Missed appointments
    Detention charges
    Lumper fees
    Fuel costs
    Tolls
    Traffic violations
    Regulatory penalties
    Any indirect, incidental, or consequential damages arising from transportation services performed by the Carrier
    Carrier remains solely responsible for transportation operations and compliance with all applicable laws.


    10. GOVERNING LAW
    This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Oklahoma unless the parties agree otherwise in writing.


    11. ELECTRONIC SIGNATURES
    Carrier agrees that electronic signatures, electronic records, and electronically submitted documents are valid and legally binding to the fullest extent permitted by applicable law.


    12. ENTIRE AGREEMENT
    This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between GraniteSky Dispatching and the Carrier regarding dispatch services and supersedes any prior verbal or written agreements relating to those services.

    Any amendments must be made in writing and agreed to by both parties.
