About GraniteSky

Built to Support the Backbone of American Freight.

GraniteSky Dispatching exists to help independent owner-operators and small fleets grow with dependable dispatching, honest communication, and long-term partnerships.

Helping Drivers Stay Focused on the Road

GraniteSky Dispatching was founded with one goal: helping independent truck drivers and small fleets succeed.


We understand that every load matters and every mile impacts your business. That's why we work every day to secure quality freight, negotiate competitive rates, reduce deadhead miles, and provide responsive support throughout every trip.


Whether you're running one truck or managing a growing fleet, our mission is simple: keep your trucks moving while building relationships based on trust, professionalism, and reliability.
